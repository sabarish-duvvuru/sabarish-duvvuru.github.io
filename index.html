How I use AI tools

AI is a workflow assistant, not a replacement for engineering judgment.

01

Extract

Convert meeting notes, emails, PDFs, tracker rows, and technical notes into structured actions and risks.

02

Organize

Separate fact, assumption, owner, due date, evidence needed, decision needed, and next check.

03

Summarize

Produce concise updates for engineering reviews, leadership alignment, and follow-up planning.

04

Validate

Keep outputs review-controlled: AI drafts the view; engineering judgment accepts or rejects it.
